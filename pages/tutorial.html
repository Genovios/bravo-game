Tutorial Page

THIS IS A PUNCHING BAG ->
On the game page, use your mouse to click the Punching Bag - in other words,"punch" it. For every punch you give the Punching Bag, you gain "Power" equal to your Punching Strength. This Power can be used to buy upgrades from Training, either increasing your Punching Strength or enabling and upgrading Auto Punching.

Key Terms

  • Punching Bag:
    Main object of the game; click it to earn Power!
  • Power:
    Currency gained from punching the punching bag, used to buy upgrades from Training.
  • Punching Strength:
    The current amount of Power you will get from punching, can be upgraded.
  • Auto Punching:
    Gain a passive amount of Power every second, can be upgraded.
  • Lifetime Clicks:
    The amount of times you've clicked - or punched - the punching bag.
